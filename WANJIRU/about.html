About KidsCare Kenya

KidsCare Kenya is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting children and schools in developing countries. Our mission is to create a better future for children by promoting sustainable growth and development.

We believe in making a positive impact by:

  • Aiding disadvantaged children by providing essential support and resources.
  • Curating learning and networking opportunities to empower children and educators.
  • Providing funding and coaching to scale innovations in partnership with governments.
  • Engaging in strategic policy advocacy to address systemic challenges.

Through our collaborative efforts, we aim to increase awareness, promote our services, and expand our reach to make a lasting difference in the lives of children and communities.
